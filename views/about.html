It’s time to update the college experience. HearYe is a mobile application designed to centralize all campus events.

  • The problem

    Current channels students use to interact with campus events are inadequate.

  • The solution

    A consolidated platform to browse or create events on campus.

  • What’s going on around campus?

    Scroll through our clean, easy to view feed to browse a comprehensive list of events, or filter it to your style.

  • HearYe helps students and organizations advertise their gatherings.

    With our simple event creation interface, groups and individuals alike can easily reach out to the entire campus.

Interact

After showing interest in or selecting an event, users have multiple options of interaction, such as sharing the event with friends, or messaging the planner for additional information.
